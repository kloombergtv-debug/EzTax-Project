SSL Certificate Warning Solution

The SSL warning you're seeing is normal for Replit development URLs. Here are safe ways to access your application:

Method 1: Chrome - Advanced

When you see the SSL warning:

  1. Click "Advanced" on the warning page
  2. Click "Proceed to [domain] (unsafe)"
  3. Your EzTax app will load normally
Open EzTax App

Method 2: Deploy for SSL

Deploy your application to get a proper SSL certificate:

  1. Go back to Replit
  2. Click the "Deploy" button
  3. Your app will get a secure .replit.app domain
