Method 1: Direct Browser Access

Click the button below to open your EzTax application in a new tab. If you see an SSL warning, click "Advanced" then "Proceed to site".

Open EzTax Application

Method 2: Chrome Flags (One-time setup)

For permanent access without SSL warnings:

  1. Open Chrome and go to: chrome://flags/#allow-insecure-localhost
  2. Enable "Allow invalid certificates for resources loaded from localhost"
  3. Restart Chrome
  4. Access your application normally
